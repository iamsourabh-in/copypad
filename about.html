About CopyPad

CopyPad is a simple and efficient tool designed to streamline your workflow. It allows you to save frequently used text snippets and copy them to your clipboard with a single click, eliminating the need for manual selection.

Key Features:

  • Instant Copy: Instantly copy any text you saved directly, rather than manually selecting it with the cursor.
  • Organize with Categories: Create your own categories to work with and keep your copy pad organized.
  • Simple Interface: A clean and intuitive interface that is easy to use.
